Social Media Marketing Case Study
Social Media
Legal / Immigration

How we grew an immigration firm's social following by 400% and increased leads by 40%

AMK Global · Canada & UK

How we grew an immigration firm's social following by 400 percent and increased leads by 40 percent by combining clearer branding, stronger Meta content, and faster community management.

Headline result
400% audience growth

Meta shifted from a basic presence into a stronger brand-discovery channel for an immigration and recruitment firm serving Canada and the UK.

Content reach
10K+ Facebook growth

Consistent platform-specific content and brand direction expanded AMK Global's reach and follower growth across Meta.

Buyer intent signal
40% lead lift

More consistent content, better aesthetics, and stronger social engagement translated into more high-quality organic leads.

01

Challenge

AMK Global needed social media to do more than simply stay active. As a Canada- and UK-based immigration and recruitment company helping people study, settle, work, and build businesses abroad, the brand needed a clearer Meta strategy that could grow the audience, improve brand presentation, educate prospects, and convert attention into qualified leads.

02

Strategy / what we did

Positioned the brand as a trusted immigration partner, not just another service page

The messaging was shaped around clarity, trust, and practical value so the content spoke to students, professionals, families, and employers exploring opportunities abroad.

Built a Meta engine around growth, aesthetics, and lead capture

Branding, content planning, and social engagement systems were aligned so attention could move naturally into follower growth, stronger perception, and inbound lead conversations.

  • Conducted competitor research to identify content opportunities and sharper positioning on Meta platforms.
  • Built a full branding guide covering colors, fonts, and visual language to improve Instagram and Facebook consistency.
  • Maintained a consistent posting schedule with educational and engagement-led content.
  • Set up a streamlined system for handling inbox messages, comments, and social interactions so leads were not missed.
03

Execution breakdown

Expertise framed for decision-makers

The brand identity and content were anchored in trust and clarity so prospective clients could quickly understand what AMK Global does and why the firm was worth engaging.

Content built recurring visibility and follower momentum

Instagram and Facebook posts created sustained growth, better engagement, and stronger top-of-funnel awareness instead of relying on scattered updates.

Community management translated attention into leads

Faster response handling across comments, DMs, and interactions helped convert audience interest into a steadier stream of high-quality organic leads.

04

Key metrics / outcomes

The result was social media becoming a real growth and lead-generation channel, not just a posting routine. Branding, content quality, follower growth, and engagement systems reinforced each other, helping AMK Global scale audience size and improve lead flow through Meta platforms.

Meta audience growth
400%
The audience base grew sharply across Instagram and Facebook, showing that the new content system was compounding over time.
Organic leads increase
40%
Higher-quality organic leads increased through social media as brand presentation and response systems improved.
Facebook audience growth
10K+
Facebook audience size increased by roughly ten thousand, validating stronger distribution and content-market fit.
Instagram audience growth
2.5K+
Instagram audience size increased by roughly two and a half thousand while supporting stronger brand recall and engagement.
  • Grew follower base by 400 percent across Meta platforms.
  • Increased high-quality organic leads from social media by 40 percent.
  • Expanded the Facebook audience by around 10,000 followers.
  • Expanded the Instagram audience by around 2,500 followers.
